Meet Other Founders

It can be difficult meeting other startup founders. Coffee Roulette arranges for you to meet another startup founder for coffee.

How it works

Once a fortnight we randomly pair you up with another founder. We email you each other's details and then you arrange between you when and where to meet up for coffee. You never get paired with the same person twice.

Anything else?

  • Please don't apply if you aren't a founder.
  • What you discuss is off the record unless otherwise agreed.
  • We will never sell your data.
